Some facts about Chrissy

Christina Nicola "Chrissy" Costanza (born August 23, 1995) is an American singer, songwriter and musician. She is best known as the lead vocalist and primary songwriter of the rock band, Against The Current. She is also a YouTube personality. Her self-titled YouTube channel, where she posts vlogs and beauty tutorials, currently has over 800,000

Some facts about Dan

Dan Gow is an American guitarist best known for being a member of the breakout band Against the Current. The band is popular for its authentic musical style that has gained them a large YouTube following despite uploading only a few covers of popular hit singles. Dan, Chrissy, and Will started creating original music in 2012 and were quickly booked for World Tour
